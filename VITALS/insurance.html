Core Coverage Details

A breakdown of what's fully covered under the Sachidaanand Exclusive Plan.

Inpatient Hospitalization

Covers expenses for a minimum of 24 hours hospitalization, including room rent, nursing charges, doctor fees, and ICU costs.

Pre & Post Hospitalization

Expenses incurred for diagnostic tests, consultations, and medication up to **30 days before admission** and **60 days after discharge**.

Daycare Procedures

Coverage for all treatments and surgeries requiring less than 24 hours of hospitalization due to advanced technology.

Annual Health Check-Up

One complimentary full body health check-up is included for all members aged 18 and above.

Ambulance Cover

Emergency ambulance services are covered up to a set limit per hospitalization within the city limits.

Maternity Benefit

Coverage for maternity-related expenses, including delivery (normal or C-section), after a waiting period.
